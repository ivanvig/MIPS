** SeÃ±ales de control                                                                                                                                                               

*** DEC
    - RA
    - SHAMT

*** EX                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                    
    - ALU [4bits]                                                                                                                                                                   
    	+ 0000 -> ADD                                                                                                                                                                 
    	+ 0001 -> SUB                                                                                                                                                                 
    	+ 0010 -> AND                                                                                                                                                                 
    	+ 0011 -> OR                                                                                                                                                                  
    	+ 0100 -> XOR                                                                                                                                                                 
    	+ 0101 -> NOR                                                                                                                                                                 
    	+ 0110 -> SRL                                                                                                                                                                 
    	+ 0111 -> SLL                                                                                                                                                                 
    	+ 1000 -> SRA                                                                                                                                                                 
    	+ 1001 -> SLA                                                                                                                                                                 
    	+ 1010 -> SLT                                                                                                                                                                 
    	+ 1011 -> LUI
    - B/I
    - S/U

*** MEM
    
    - RE
    - WE
    - S/U
    - DATA_SIZE [2bits]
      + 00 -> byte
      + 01 -> half-word
      + 10 -> word
      + 11 -> NO!

*** WB

    - REG_WE
    - MEM/ALU
    - DATA/PC

** Estructura de bits de control

   |6        |2    |1    |0      |
   | ALUCTRL | B/I | S/U | SHAMT |
   
*** MEM (5 bits)
   |4	  |3	 |2    |1			 |
   | RE | WE | S/U | DSIZE |
   
*** WB (8 bits)
   |7		  |2			 |1				 |0				 |
   | DEST | REG_WE | MEM/ALU | DATA/PC |
